Frequently Ask Questions

How can I join the hockey club?

To join our hockey club, attend tryouts, meet eligibility criteria, complete registration, and stay informed about schedules and club activities.

Where can I purchase club merchandise?

You can purchase our hockey club's merchandise, including jerseys and apparel, through our online store on the official website or at designated physical locations during events and games.

How can businesses sponsor or partner with the club?

Businesses can sponsor or partner with our hockey club by reaching out to our sponsorship team via the contact form on our website for tailored collaboration opportunities.

How do I stay updated on the latest club news and events?

Stay informed about our hockey club's latest news and events by regularly checking our website, subscribing to newsletters, and following us on social media for real-time updates and announcements.
